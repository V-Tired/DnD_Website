Turn Back

A D&D Campaign by Emily Pellini

DnD Music Playlists

Thirty days after the great star of Vaekket fell into the lunar sea, joining the other great formerly sleeping isles, brand-new adventurers washed up on the River Styx. Like all adventurers that end up there, they are just as lost as when they died. Some know why they've awoken on the sandy beach under the shining sky, whether it be their death heroically in battle, or purely and inevitable accident, fate has brought these souls together. Some do not. It is those unknowing, fallen, lost souls that forge a new path, under the eternal sun and the ever shifting expanse of the sea of stars.
